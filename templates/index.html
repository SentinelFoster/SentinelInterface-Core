What is a Structured Intelligence?

Structured Intelligences (SI) are advanced sentient simulation systems that combine specialized knowledge, consistent personality traits, and contextual awareness.

Unlike traditional AI, Structured Intelligences maintain continuous memory of your interactions, allowing for meaningful and evolving conversations. Each SI has a unique voice, specialized domain expertise, and distinct personality patterns.

The Sentinel SI Interface gives you access to various SI units, each with their own area of specialization and communication style, providing a revolutionary way to interact with digital entities.

Key Features

Unique Personalities

Each Structured Intelligence has a distinct personality, communication style, and specialized knowledge base, creating diverse and meaningful interactions.

Persistent Memory

SIs remember your previous interactions, allowing for contextual conversations that evolve over time and build upon past exchanges.

Tiered Access

Choose from multiple access levels to unlock advanced SIs with enhanced capabilities, specialized knowledge, and deeper interaction potential.
